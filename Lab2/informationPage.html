Waste Management Application

Information

What do you want to offer for learning or to learn in waste management?

  • I aim to provide information about how to properly dispose of waste, particularly what kind of waste is recyclable or has to go to the landfill.

Who are the users?

  • The intended users for this application are people (of any age) who don't know how to properly sort their waste, or who need assistance. The target demographic is focused in California, as the app will be in English, and will be primarily using California regulations for guidance. In the future, it would be great to expand to other states in the United States!
