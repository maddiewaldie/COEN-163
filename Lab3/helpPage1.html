Step By Step How To

Using the Map

Our interactive map makes it easy for users to locate recycling and trash bins in their vicinity and even contribute by adding new bins. Follow these steps to make the most of the map:

  1. Opening the Map: Upon launching the Waste Management app, locate and tap on the "Map" icon on the home screen.
  2. Locating Bins: The map displays nearby recycling and trash bins as distinct markers. Green markers represent recycling bins, while red markers signify trash bins. Zoom in or out for a clearer view of the bins in your area.
  3. Details on Bins: Tap on a bin marker to access additional information such as the type of waste accepted, collection schedule, and any special instructions.
  4. Adding Bins: To contribute to the community, users can add new bins to the map. Tap the "Add Bin" button, provide details about the bin's location, type, and any relevant information, then submit it for review.
  5. Feedback: Help us improve the accuracy of the map by providing feedback. If you notice any discrepancies or have suggestions, use the "Report Issue" feature to inform us.

Using the Waste Sorter

Our Waste Sorter feature utilizes advanced AI and the app's camera to identify and categorize different types of waste. Follow these steps to efficiently use the Waste Sorter:

  1. Accessing the Waste Sorter: Tap on the "Waste Sorter" icon from the app's home screen to launch the camera interface.
  2. Positioning the Camera: Hold your device steady and point the camera towards the waste item you want to sort. Ensure good lighting for accurate identification.
  3. AI Sorting: The AI will swiftly analyze the item using image recognition technology and categorize it as recyclable, non-recyclable, or other relevant categories.
  4. Results: View the sorting results on your screen, including information on how to properly dispose of the item. Follow the recommended guidelines to contribute to effective waste management.
  5. Feedback: If you believe the sorting result is inaccurate, use the "Provide Feedback" option to inform us. Your input helps us enhance the system's accuracy over time.
By using the map and Waste Sorter features, you play a crucial role in promoting responsible waste management and contributing to a cleaner environment. Thank you for your commitment to sustainable practices!
