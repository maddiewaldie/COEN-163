Waste Management Application

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

This page addresses some of the frequently asked questions users may have about the application.

How does the app categorize my waste?

  • The app utilizes a highly trained machine learning model to categorize your trash into two categories – landfill waste and recyclables. This model runs on the feed of your device's camera, analyzing the trash within its view.

Is there a way to report if the application gives me incorrect information?

  • Yes! If the application provides improper waste sorting guidance (e.g. tells you to throw out something you know can be recycled), you can press the report button (that looks like this ⚠️). The AI model will learn from your help! Additionally, if a trash can or recycling bin appears on the map, but it isn't there in real life, you can use the form below to contact us, and we'll investigate!

How does the app know where trash and recycling bins are?

  • The app uses a combination of our own TrashMap database, as well as crowdsourcing information to determine where trash and recycling bins are located.

Because the app uses my location and my camera, is all of my information secure?

  • Yes! All of your informoation is secure! The camera feed is encrypted and run through the machine learning model on device, and your location is only used for the map. We don't collect or store any of your personal information.
